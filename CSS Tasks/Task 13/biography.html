John Doe

A John Doe order is a type of legal order that allows a person or entity to take legal action against an unknown party or parties. In English A John Doe order is a type of legal order that allows a person or entity to take legal action against an unknown party or parties. This type of order is often used when the identity of the person or entity being sued is not known at the time when the legal action is being taken.
